Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-01-01

Welcome to YouRe. This Privacy Policy explains how YGY LABS ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information about you when you use our mobile application ("Service").

1. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Information You Provide to Us: This includes information you provide when you create an account, such as your email address and password. It also includes content you create, such as uploaded books and voice recordings made using the "Parent Voice Recording" feature.
  • Information We Collect Automatically: When you use our Service, we automatically collect certain information, including your device type, IP address, operating system, and usage data like reading progress, mission completion, and interactions with app features.
  • Information from Third Parties: If you sign in through a third-party service (like Apple or Google), we receive information from that service, such as your basic profile information. We also use third-party services for payment processing (like the App Store), and while we don't receive your full payment details, we get confirmation of your purchases.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our Service.
  • Create and manage your account and synchronize your data across devices.
  • Process your subscriptions and in-app purchases.
  • Personalize your experience, such as tracking your reading achievements.
  • Communicate with you, including sending service-related notifications.
  • Monitor and analyze trends and usage to improve the user experience.
  • Protect the security and integrity of our Service.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with the following third parties:

  • Service Providers: We use third-party service providers who need access to your information to perform services on our behalf, such as cloud hosting and analytics (e.g., Firebase).
  • For Legal Reasons: We may disclose your information if we believe that disclosure is necessary to comply with a law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.

4. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with the Service. We will delete your information upon your request or when it is no longer needed for the purposes for which it was collected.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have rights over your personal information, including:

  • Accessing and Updating Your Information: You can review and update your account information through your profile settings.
  • Account Deletion: You have the right to delete your account at any time. When you delete your account, we will permanently delete your personal information, including your profile, created books, and voice recordings. Please note this action is irreversible. You can find the account deletion option in the app's settings/profile page.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for unsupervised use by children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). The "Parent Voice Recording" feature is designed for parents or legal guardians to create recordings for their children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without parental consent.

7. Security

We take reasonable measures to help protect your information from loss, theft, misuse, and unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • By email: contact@ygylabs.com

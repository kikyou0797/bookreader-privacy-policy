The rhythm

A tiny loop kids understand in seconds.

StarWise is built around a simple behavioral loop: visible goals, parent confirmation, meaningful rewards, and a history that stays honest when routines change.

01

Set a gentle plan

Choose habits, one-time tasks, star values, and rewards that match your family instead of someone else's chore chart.

02

Approve the win

Kids can see the plan, but stars are awarded only after parent approval with biometrics or PIN. Motivation without loopholes.

03

Watch progress compound

Stars become a language for effort, patience, and tradeoffs, from small treats to long-term goals.

Why it feels different

Parent-grade controls

Sweet on the surface. Serious underneath.

Behind the playful interface is a system designed for real family boundaries: approval gates, purchase locks, accurate history, and private local-first data.

Lock

Parent Mode is protected by biometrics or PIN, with a separate purchase lock option for rewards.

Ledger

Stars are handled as transactions, so the balance stays explainable and trustworthy.

History

Past daily plans are frozen snapshots, so old records do not change when habits are edited later.

Parent approval

Confirm finished tasks

A calm control center for reviewing today's goals and keeping the star economy fair.

Reward design

Small treats, big goals

Create repeatable rewards, one-time milestones, and a main target that kids can rally around.

Inside the app

A soft little universe for effort.

Family-first privacy

No child account. No kid ad profile. No weirdness.

StarWise Kids is designed to work locally by default. Optional sync and purchases are parent-controlled, and Child Mode stays focused on the child experience.
